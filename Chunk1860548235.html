Servicing

Task: 122171

Left and Right Flap Connecting, Interconnecting and Pushrods.
Note:

If aircraft operates in a seacoast environment, rod ends may require more frequent lubrication. When aircraft is washed or de-iced, rod ends may require lubrication.

  1. Disconnect rod ends, clean and inspect rod end ball for condition, corrosion and pitting.
  2. Rotate rod end ball and check for roughness. If roughness is present and persists after lubrication, replace rod end.
  3. Lubricate using MIL-L-7870 oil or equivalent. Rotate ball ends to evenly disperse lubricant. Wipe off excess.
    Note:

    Lubrication is not required if sealed bearings are installed.

  4. Connect rod ends in accordance with the 208 Series Maintenance Manual, Chapter 27-50.
TASK COMPLETE

Task: 122172

Note:

Beginning with aircraft serial number 20800161 and 208B0190 and aircraft modified per SNL89-17 have sealed pivot bearings installed and do not require lubrication. To identify these bellcranks, measure the bellcrank mount tube outside diameter (See A-A in Figure 1 below). The NEW style bellcrank tube is 1.00". The OLD style bellcrank tube outside diameter is 0.687" and MUST be removed for lubrication.

Right and Left Outboard Flap Bellcrank Bearings Requiring Lubrication.
  1. Remove bellcranks from wing.
  2. Remove bearings, clean and inspect for condition, pitting and corrosion.
    Note:

    If bearing is found defective, consider replacing bellcrank with sealed bearings in accordance with SNL89-17.

  3. If bearing passes inspection, lubricate with MIL-G-81322 grease or equivalent.
  4. Replace bearing in bellcrank and install bellcrank in accordance with the 208 Series Maintenance Manual, Chapter 27-50.
TASK COMPLETE

Task: 122174

Left and Right Spoiler Hinge Assemblies.
  1. Wipe clean and spray lubricate with high quality silicon spray.
  2. Wipe off excess.

Task: 122176

Left and Right Flap Track and Roller Assemblies.
  1. Wipe clean and lubricate with high quality silicon spray.
  2. Wipe off excess.
TASK COMPLETE

Task: 122177

Left and Right Aileron & Spoiler Pushrods.
  1. Disconnect pushrods, clean rod ends, inspect for condition, corrosion and pitting.
  2. Lubricate by hand using MIL-G-21164 grease or equivalent.
  3. Connect rod ends in accordance with the 208 Series Maintenance Manual, Chapter 27-10.
TASK COMPLETE
