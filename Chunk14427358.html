Environmental Systems

Task: 212102

Wing Mounted Ventilation System.
  1. Inspect mounting points and flanges for cracks.
  2. Inspect control cable and arm at cabin air control valve for condition and security.
  3. Inspect blower(s) (if installed) for condition and security of installation.
  4. Inspect wiring at blower for condition and security.
TASK COMPLETE

Task: 212104

Note:

Prior to removing the blower, perform an operational check for excessive vibration or noises.

Ventilating Blower (if installed).
  1. Remove blower(s) and inspect blower(s) fan/wheel for damage.
  2. Inspect motor shaft bearing for excessive end-play. Rotate fan by hand and listen for unusual noises.
TASK COMPLETE
